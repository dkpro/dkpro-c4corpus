Idol Levels Explained
Bronze Idol - Health Walker - 200 Minutes: Walking an average of 30 minutes or more per day has been shown by research to reduce your risks for many health problems and may increase your healthy years of life. Reduce your risk of heart disease, diabetes, stroke, and cancer. Bronze Diploma
Silver Idol - Fitness Walker - 400 Minutes: Step it up to 60 minutes per day and you will be burning extra calories to help lose fat or keep it off, in addition to the health benefits earned at the Bronze Idol level. Walking for 45 minutes to an hour gives your body incentive to burn fat and to build muscle. Silver Diploma
Gold Idol - Athletic Walker - 600 Minutes: This level is achieved by walking several longer walks each week, such as for marathon training. Gold Diploma
