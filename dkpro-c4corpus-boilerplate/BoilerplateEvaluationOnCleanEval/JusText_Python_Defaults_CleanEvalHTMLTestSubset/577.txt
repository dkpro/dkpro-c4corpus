<p> Organisations across Australia are increasingly moving
towards external service providers for the provision of IT services. As a
result, contract management is becoming one of the core activities of overall IT
management. With 97% of Australian organisations outsourcing at least some
aspect of their IT services and nearly 30% of the IT budget on average, contract
planning and management has become one of the core competencies of any IT
responsibility.
